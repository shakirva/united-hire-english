Welcome to United Hire!

United Hire has an extensive track record in advising and supporting foreign skilled workers as well as excellent expert knowledge about integration processes and the role of language, education, and perspectives in the integration of foreign skilled workers into the German labor market.

Together, we strive to do justice to this socio-politically important field and guarantee the highest quality in our service offerings. We specialize in executive services in placements, training courses (Ausbildung), study courses, and visa processing, particularly in the healthcare sector. We provide on-site training and course facilities to ensure comprehensive preparation for our candidates.

United Hire was founded to bridge the gap between healthcare professionals aspiring to study or work and healthcare organizations, matching them perfectly and integrating them into the German labor market. Our goal is to address the significant shortage of skilled nursing staff in Germany while opening new career prospects for healthcare professionals.

With us, employers and healthcare professionals receive everything from a single source. This means we organize and manage everything from the first interview with medical staff and the employer to the start of employment — including language training, recognition of qualifications, different aspects of residence law, entry into Germany, accommodation search, qualification, and placement.

Why Choose Us

Our aim is to cover the long-term demand for skilled nursing staff in Germany. Nursing staff, students, employers, and those in need of care shall be provided with optimal service.

Trust and Fairness

Dealing with all involved parties on the basis of trust and fairness is essential to our approach. We focus on long-term solutions for staffing needs.

Transparency

The procedure is clearly presented to nursing staff and students from the beginning, ensuring clarity on the process duration and content at every stage.

Unbureaucratic

We handle everything – from German courses to visa processing, entry into Germany, and finding accommodation, making the process easy for all involved.

Responsible
