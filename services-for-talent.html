Services for Talents

At United Hire, we offer a comprehensive range of services to help individuals realize their dreams of living, studying, and working in Germany. Our expert team provides support from Ausbildung courses to job placement, ensuring a smooth and stress-free transition.

Ausbildung

Germany is known for its dual education system, which is typically structured as three-and-a-half-year vocational programs. It optimally combines theoretical knowledge provided by vocational schools with practical skills acquired through training in companies.

Jobs in Germany

We specialize in placing talented professionals from Asia in Healthcare sector in Germany. With a focus on innovation, we are dedicated to building bridges to enriching and exciting career opportunities for individuals looking to expand their professional horizons in healthcare sector.

Federal Voluntary Services

The Federal Voluntary Service (BFD) is a unique opportunity for people to engage in meaningful voluntary service across Germany. It offers the chance to work in various fields such as education, health, environment, and social services, gaining valuable experience while making a significant contribution to society.
